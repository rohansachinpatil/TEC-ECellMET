About TEC

The Technical Excellence Challenge (TEC) is a premier college-level competition platform designed to foster innovation, entrepreneurship, and technical excellence among students across multiple institutions.

This 3-month intensive program challenges teams to transform their innovative ideas into viable solutions through structured phases, mentorship, and real-world problem-solving.
